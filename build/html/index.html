Welcome to qScope’s documentation!

Q-Scope is a framework for interactive visualization using tangible objects on a physical table. It is used as a decision support tool for participation processes in energy transition research. The framework is based on MIT’s CityScope platform and was developed by the Department of Resilient Energy Systems at the University of Bremen.

The code can be found on GitHub.
