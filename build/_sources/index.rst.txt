Welcome to qScope's documentation!
==================================

The Q-Scope is a decision support tool, based on `MIT's CityScope platform <https://cityscope.media.mit.edu/>`_. Q-Scope was developed by the `Department of Resilient Energy Systems <https://www.uni-bremen.de/res>`_ at the University of Bremen.`
